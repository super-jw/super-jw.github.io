I am a Ph.D. student at Trustworthy Machine Learning and Reasoning (TMLR) group in the Department of Computer Science, Hong Kong Baptist University (HKBU), advised by Prof. Bo Han. Previously, I received my MPhil Eng. degree and Bachelor Eng. degree from Xi'an Jiaotong University. My research interests focus on trustworthy machine learning, particularly in finding the vulnerability (e.g., adversarial attack), improving the robustness (e.g., test-time adaptation), and promoting the application (e.g., GUI agents) of deep neural networks, so as to construct responsible AI and benefit social goods and safety.

I am always open to discussions and possible collaborations. Please feel free to email me if you would like to chat.
